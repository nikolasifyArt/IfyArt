Workout Plan

Pull-Push Workout Plan

Day 1: Pull (Back & Biceps Focus)

Warm-up (5-10 minutes):

  • Dynamic stretches (arm circles, leg swings, torso twists)
  • Light cardio (jumping jacks, high knees)
  • Low-weight sets of the first exercise

Workout:

  • Deadlifts – 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Pull-Ups (or Lat Pulldown) – 4 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Barbell Rows (or Dumbbell Rows) – 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Face Pulls – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Barbell or Dumbbell Curls – 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Hammer Curls – 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Cardio (15 minutes):

  • Cat-Cow Stretch (Lower Back & Core) – 2 minutes
  • Arm Swings (Cross-Body) – 2 minutes
  • Dynamic Shoulder Rolls – 2 minutes
  • Scapular Wall Slides – 2 minutes
  • Bird-Dogs (Lower Back & Core) – 3 minutes
  • Face Pull Band Stretch – 2 minutes
  • Standing Chest Opener with Twist – 2 minutes

Cooldown (5-10 minutes):

  • Child's Pose with Side Stretch – 1-2 minutes
  • Thread the Needle Stretch – 1-2 minutes
  • Standing Forward Fold with Shoulder Stretch – 1-2 minutes
  • Seated Spinal Twist – 1-2 minutes
  • Doorway Chest Stretch – 1-2 minutes
  • Neck Stretch with Shoulder Roll – 1 minute

Day 2: Push (Chest, Shoulders & Triceps Focus)

Warm-up (5-10 minutes):

  • Dynamic stretches (arm circles, leg swings, torso twists)
  • Light cardio (jumping jacks, high knees)
  • Low-weight sets of the first exercise

Workout:

  • Bench Press (Barbell or Dumbbell) – 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Press – 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Overhead Press (Barbell or Dumbbell) – 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Lateral Raises – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Tricep Dips (or Putdowns) – 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Skull Crushers (or Overhead Triceps Extension) – 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Cardio (15 minutes):

  • Cat-Cow Stretch – 2 minutes
  • Wall Push-Ups – 2 minutes
  • Dynamic Chest Fly Stretch – 2 minutes
  • Overhead Arm Stretch with Side Bend – 2 minutes
  • Reverse Plank Hold (Lower Back & Shoulders) – 2 minutes
  • T-Spine Rotations – 3 minutes

Cooldown (5-10 minutes):

  • Child's Pose with Side Stretch – 1-2 minutes
  • Thread the Needle Stretch – 1-2 minutes
  • Standing Forward Fold with Shoulder Stretch – 1-2 minutes
  • Seated Spinal Twist – 1-2 minutes
  • Doorway Chest Stretch – 1-2 minutes
  • Neck Stretch with Shoulder Roll – 1 minute

Day 3: Rest or Active Recovery

Light cardio (20-30 minutes walking or cycling)

Mobility work or yoga for flexibility

Foam rolling or stretching

Day 4: Pull (Lower Back & Posterior Chain Focus)

Warm-up (5-10 minutes):

  • Dynamic stretches (arm circles, leg swings, torso twists)
  • Light cardio (jumping jacks, high knees)
  • Low-weight sets of the first exercise

Workout:

  • Romanian Deadlifts – 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Chin-Ups (or Weighted Chin-Ups) – 4 sets of 6-10 reps
  • Seated Cable Rows – 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Shrugs (Barbell or Dumbbell) – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Concentration Curls – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Preacher Curls – 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Cardio (15 minutes):

  • (Repeat the Day 1 Cardio routine)

Cooldown (5-10 minutes):

  • (Repeat the Cooldown routine)

Day 5: Push (Chest & Triceps Focus)

Warm-up (5-10 minutes):

  • Dynamic stretches (arm circles, leg swings, torso twists)
  • Light cardio (jumping jacks, high knees)
  • Low-weight sets of the first exercise

Workout:

  • Incline Barbell Press – 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Flat Dumbbell Press – 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Arnold Press – 4 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Chest Fly (Cable or Dumbbell) – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Close-Grip Bench Press – 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Overhead Triceps Extensions – 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Cardio (15 minutes):

  • (Repeat the Day 2 Cardio routine)

Cooldown (5-10 minutes):

  • (Repeat the Cooldown routine)
